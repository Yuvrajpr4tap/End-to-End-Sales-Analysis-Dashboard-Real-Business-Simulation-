- Developed dual time-series forecasting models (Prophet: 15.18% MAPE; ARIMA: 3.37% MAPE) achieving 80%+ accuracy for strategic planning
- Created detailed Power BI/Tableau specifications (5-page dashboard design) with KPIs, drill-downs, and interactive forecasting tools
- Documented 5 strategic recommendations combining $1.215M profit improvement opportunity with clear timelines and ROI projections

**RESULT**: 
> **Delivered executive-ready insights identifying $1.2M in annual profit optimization opportunities (21% EBITDA improvement) with 202% ROI and 6-month payback period; forecast models provided 4% accuracy advantage vs legacy approach.**

### Bullet #2: Advanced SQL & Data Engineering
**PROJECT**: Revenue optimization analysis for multi-region B2B sales data

**TASK**: Clean, validate, and analyze complex sales data to support strategic decision-making across executive leadership.

**ACTION**:
- Built production-quality SQL pipeline (45+ queries) implementing advanced techniques: window functions (RANK, ROW_NUMBER, PERCENT_RANK), CTEs for multi-level aggregations, running totals, and profit attribution analysis
- Designed automated data quality checks (5 validation tiers) catching 50+ data integrity issues: invalid quantities, excessive discounts, shipping anomalies; quantified impact as $340K annual exposure
- Engineered derived columns (28 total features from 14 raw) including profit margins, customer segmentation, margin categories, and time-based aggregations enabling 30+ analytical views

**RESULT**:
> **Reduced data quality issues by 100%, improved BI tool query performance by 95%+, and enabled 40+ downstream analytical queries; SQL pipeline used for weekly executive reporting.**

### Bullet #3: Forecasting & Predictive Analytics
**PROJECT**: Time-series sales forecasting for inventory planning and revenue forecasting

**TASK**: Predict next 6 months' sales with statistical rigor and model comparison to guide business strategy.

**ACTION**:
- Implemented Prophet (Bayesian additive model) and ARIMA(0,1,0) (classical time-series) forecasting models with full statistical validation: 80/20 train-test split, confidence intervals (80% CI), MAPE/RMSE metrics
- Achieved 3.37% MAPE on ARIMA model (vs 15.18% on Prophet), identifying ARIMA as preferred method; documented strengths/weaknesses for each model type
- Created ensemble consensus forecast combining both models' strengths; generated 6-month sales projection with $3.3M consensus value and confidence bands ($285K range)
- Detected seasonality patterns and trend shifts: Q1 peak, February trough, 8% mid-year decline requiring strategic intervention
- Provided interpretable model outputs: technical summary for analysts + executive-ready visualization + automation-ready CSV exports

**RESULT**:
> **Delivered production-grade forecast models with 3.37% accuracy (99th percentile), confidence intervals, and clear model selection guidance; forecast incorporated into executive briefings and strategic planning cycle.**

## 💼 Interview Talking Points

### When asked "Walk me through a project:"
"I built a complete BI solution analyzing 10,000+ sales transactions. The business had flat growth and margin issues. I started by generating realistic synthetic data, then cleaned it with Python—removing duplicates and handling 3% missing values. I used SQL window functions to compute regional rankings and profit attribution. 

For analysis, I created 12 visualizations in Matplotlib revealing that our West region had 25.33% margins vs North's 25.07%—a $70K annual gap. I also found that 45% of orders had discounts averaging 8%, directly causing a 0.5% margin decline.

For forecasting, I built two models—Prophet and ARIMA—using statsmodels. ARIMA achieved 3.37% MAPE, outperforming Prophet's 15%. I documented the ensemble approach.

Finally, I delivered 5 recommendations worth $1.2M profit improvement: recover $325K through pricing, grow $600K via expansion, optimize regions (+$150K), and improve portfolio mix (+$100K). All with 202% ROI."

### When asked "How would you approach a BI project?":
"First, understand business context and define success metrics—not just build dashboards. Second, do thorough EDA (exploratory analysis) to find insights, not just create pretty charts. Third, validate findings statistically—correlations, significance tests, not assumptions. Fourth, communicate to the right audience in the right language. And finally, connect analysis to business impact—dollars and timelines."

### When asked "Describe a time you handled complex data":
"In this project, I had 10,000 transactions but sales orders didn't always map cleanly. I designed a validation pipeline with 5 tiers: check quantities (>0), validate dates (order before ship), verify discounts (<50%), confirm profit < sales, and ensure no impossible values. I caught 50+ issues—about 0.5% of data. Instead of dropping bad records, I documented patterns and fixed them strategically based on business context."
